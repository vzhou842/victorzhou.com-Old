CLOAK


Securely and seamlessly hiding sensitive personal information in plain sight.





Cloak allows you to take all of the sensitive personal information, like passwords, account numbers, and your SSN, that you might store on your phone and hide in an encrypted form within an image on your phone.



The changes made on your image aren't visible to the naked human eye, but Cloak can read and decrypt the message at anytime.



That means you can securely store all kinds of personal information right on your phone, where it's most convenient. Just remember which picture in your Camera Roll you Cloaked, and decrypt it using the app whenever you need it. If your phone ever gets stolen, your personal information will be safe -- plus, you'll be able to recover all of that information if you backed up your Cloaked image to the cloud.
