Enroute Innovations, LLC

This is just a placeholder website for the company while we get the actual website up and running, so forgive us if it's a bit rough around the edges.

The main focus for Enroute Innovations, LLC will be our app/service called "ontheway". We're currently developing iOS, Android, and web versions of this app. What it basically will do is link 'requesters', e.g. people who want a sandwich but don't want to get it themselves, with 'deliverers', e.g. people who want to make a bit of extra money and/or are already "ontheway" to that store or area anyways. The app will provide a simple, intuitive, and sleek interface to connect these two groups of people and give everybody what they want.
