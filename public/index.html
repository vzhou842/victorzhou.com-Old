About Me

I'm a software developer from Plano, Texas. I'm well-versed in iOS (Objective-C) and Android (Java) app development, Node.js (Javascript), R, C/C++, HTML/CSS, as well as Python, SQL, and PHP to a lesser degree. I've been a pretty avid iOS developer for some time, and have ~20 apps in the App Store totalling close to 100,000 downloads. One of the projects I'm most proud of is Ontheway, a peer-to-peer delivery network I started with a few friends for which I built the vast majority of the iOS, Android, and Node.js apps.



If you have any thoughts or comments on me or my projects, please use the Contact section. I appreciate your time and interest.
